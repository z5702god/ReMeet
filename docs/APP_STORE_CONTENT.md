## App Description

### English

Re:Meet is a smart business card management app that helps you capture, organize, and search your professional contacts effortlessly.

**Key Features:**

• **Instant Card Scanning** - Snap a photo of any business card and let AI extract all contact details automatically

• **Smart Organization** - Contacts are organized by company, tags, and meeting context so you never lose track of who you met

• **AI-Powered Search** - Find contacts using natural language. Just ask "Who did I meet at the tech conference?" or "Show me engineers from Google"

• **Meeting Context** - Add notes about where and when you met someone, making it easy to recall the context of your connections

• **Company View** - See all contacts grouped by their companies for easy reference

• **Timeline View** - Browse your contacts chronologically to remember when you met each person

**Perfect for:**
- Business professionals who network frequently
- Conference and event attendees
- Sales and business development teams
- Anyone who collects business cards

Stop losing valuable business connections. Let Re:Meet help you remember everyone you meet.

### 繁體中文

Re:Meet 是一款智能名片管理 App，幫助您輕鬆擷取、整理和搜尋專業人脈。

**主要功能：**

• **即時名片掃描** - 拍下任何名片，AI 自動擷取所有聯絡資訊

• **智能分類整理** - 依公司、標籤、會面情境分類，讓您不再遺漏重要人脈

• **AI 智能搜尋** - 用自然語言搜尋聯絡人。例如「在科技展認識的人」或「Google 的工程師」

• **會面情境記錄** - 記錄您在何時何地認識對方，輕鬆回想每段人脈的緣由

• **公司分類檢視** - 依公司分組顯示所有聯絡人，方便查閱

• **時間軸瀏覽** - 按時間順序瀏覽聯絡人，回想認識每個人的時間點

**適合對象：**
- 經常參加商務社交的專業人士
- 展覽會議參加者
- 業務開發團隊
- 任何經常收集名片的人

別再讓寶貴的商業人脈流失。讓 Re:Meet 幫您記住每一位認識的人。
